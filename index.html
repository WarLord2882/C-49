Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  1. Chameleons actually change their colour due to a number of different factors, including their mood, changes in light or temperature, or the humidity of their environment.
  2. Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  3. Unlike many lizards, chameleons cannot grow back their tails.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  2. Strange behavior! Pandas like to lick copper and iron.
  3. They have a “pseudo thumb” that helps them hold onto bamboo stems.

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. They are one of only a small number of species to pass the 'mirror test' – a test of self recognition.
  3. If you shoo a pigeon, that bird is likely to remember you and know to stay out of your way the next time you cross paths.
